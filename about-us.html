About Us

Institute of Computer Science (ICS), Satara, was founded by Mr. Sagar Desai with the vision of building a successful career for students in the field of IT. Since our inception, we have been conducting classes in various computer-related fields.

ICS stands out as an institute capable of grooming both average and brilliant students. Our dedicated, qualified, and experienced teaching staff leave no stone unturned to bring out the best in every student. Regular assignments are provided on each topic to enhance student confidence and assess their progress.

Additionally, we have guest faculty from esteemed educational institutions and the IT field. Our staff members have assisted numerous students in achieving success in their careers. Known for their personalized approach, our teachers provide individual focus to each and every student.

The Institute has become a well-established name in the coaching arena for various computer languages. We offer all the requisite facilities needed for high-standard education. Comprehensive study material, thoroughly prepared by our qualified teachers and aligned with current exam patterns, is provided to each student.
